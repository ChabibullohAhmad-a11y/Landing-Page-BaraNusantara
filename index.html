Product Specifications

Two grades, one standard of consistency

Cube & Flat Charcoal — Hookah Grade

Pressed for slow, even combustion with minimal flavor interference — the standard choice for lounges and shisha distributors across the Middle East and Europe.

  • Sizes: 22mm / 25mm cubes, flat
  • Packing: 1kg / 500g retail boxes

Pillow & Hexagonal Briquette — BBQ Grade

Denser and higher-carbon for restaurant grills and home barbecue markets — built to hold consistent temperature across long cook times.

  • Shapes: Pillow, hexagonal, cylinder
  • Packing: 1kg / 3kg / 10kg cartons
